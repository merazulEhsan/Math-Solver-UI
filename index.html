Find a root of an equation ƒ(x) = 2x3-2x-5 using Bisection Method

Solution:

Here 2x3-2x-5=0

Let, ƒ(x) = 2x3-2x-5

Iteration 1

Step 01:

ƒ(xl)×ƒ(xu) = {2 × (-2)2 + 2.4 ×(-2) + 5.5} × {2 × (-1)2 + 2.4 ×(-1) + 5.5}
= (-1.700) × 2.5
= -4.250

Step 02:

xr = x l - x u 2
= -2 + 1 2
= -1.500

Error:

e = | x u - x r x r | × 100%
= | -1 - (-1.5) -1.5 |× 100% = 14.29%

Step 03:

ƒ(xl)×ƒ(xr) = {2 × (-2)2 + 2.4 ×(-2) + 5.5} × {2 × (-1)2 + 2.4 ×(-1) + 5.5}
= (-1.700) × 2.5
= -4.250

∴ xl = xr = -1.5
